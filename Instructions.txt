Android:
Works on Phone and Tablet
Android Min Version: 6

1. Connect your android to your computer
2. Add the .apk File to your phones folder such as the download folder
3. Open the .apk file with your files manager app (If you dont have the file manager app download from google play store)
4. Open the folder where you copyed the .apk to in this example the folder is the downloads folder and open the apk.
5. Install
6. Enjoy

If you are having problems installing the app or your phone/tablet or your phone/tablet wont allow the app to be installed. Please visit these sites for more information:
